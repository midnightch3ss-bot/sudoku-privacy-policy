# sudoku-privacy-policy
Privacy Policy for Sudoku Xtreme

Effective Date: December 2025
Last Updated: December 2025

Sudoku Xtreme is developed and operated by MAMORU (“we,” “us,” or “our”).
This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use Sudoku Xtreme (the “App”).

Sudoku Xtreme is designed to be calm, honest, and respectful of your privacy. The App does not contain ads, does not use third-party analytics trackers, and does not sell personal data.

1. Overview (Plain Language)

You can use Sudoku Xtreme without creating an account.

By default, all progress and settings are stored locally on your device.

If you choose to enter an email, it is used only to associate your data on the same device.

Sudoku Xtreme does not sync data across different devices.

Optional support payments are processed by Google Play. We do not see or store your payment details.

2. Information We Collect
A. Information You Provide (Optional)

Email Address (Optional)
You may choose to provide an email address in the App. Providing an email is not required to play Sudoku Xtreme.

If provided, the email is used only to:

Associate and restore your app data on the same device

Prepare for possible account-related features in the future

At this time, the email does not enable cross-device syncing.

B. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

Sudoku Xtreme stores gameplay and preference data locally on your device, including:

Puzzle progress and in-progress games

Solve history (times, mistakes, hints used)

Daily challenge completion status

Streak data and timestamps

Difficulty progress and unlocks

App preferences and accessibility settings

Cosmetic supporter status (if applicable)

This information remains on your device unless you manually clear the App’s storage or uninstall the App.

C. Payments and Support

Sudoku Xtreme may offer optional “Support the Developer” contributions.

Payments are processed securely by Google Play Billing

We do not collect, process, or store payment card information

Google Play may provide limited purchase confirmation data to enable cosmetic supporter indicators

D. Automatically Collected Information

Sudoku Xtreme does not use:

Advertising SDKs

Third-party analytics or tracking tools

Behavioral profiling or cross-app tracking

Your device and operating system may collect basic diagnostic data according to your system settings.

3. How Information Is Used

Information is used only to:

Provide core gameplay functionality

Save progress and preferences on your device

Display statistics, streaks, and achievements

Restore local data when reopening the App on the same device

Respond to user support requests

We do not use information for advertising, tracking, or resale.

4. Email Use and Limitations

If you provide an email address:

It is optional

It is not required to play the App

It currently applies only to the same device

It does not enable cross-device syncing

If cross-device features are added in the future, the App will clearly explain the change and this Privacy Policy will be updated.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal data.

Information may be shared only in limited cases:

Service Providers: If future features require secure backend storage, this will be disclosed clearly before use.

Legal Requirements: If required by law or valid legal process.

Google Play: For payment processing and purchase validation.

6. Data Retention
Local Data

Local data remains on your device until you:

Clear the App’s storage, or

Uninstall the App

Email Data

If stored locally, the email remains on the device until App data is cleared. If backend storage is introduced in the future, retention policies will be updated accordingly.

7. Your Choices

You may:

Use the App without providing any personal information

Remove all stored data by clearing App storage or uninstalling

Choose whether or not to provide an email

8. Children’s Privacy

Sudoku Xtreme does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

9. Security

We take reasonable steps to minimize data collection and protect user information:

No ads or trackers

No storage of payment card data

Local-first data design

No system is completely secure, but we aim to reduce risk by limiting what data is collected.

10. International Users

Sudoku Xtreme may be used worldwide. At present, data is stored locally on your device. If future features involve cloud storage, this policy will be updated.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the App’s functionality changes. The “Last Updated” date will be revised accordingly.
